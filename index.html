Project specification

Drag the cards to move them

In my project, I want to show everyone that Shibuya can be an amazing destination, through a travel website.

I shall give you a general idea about Shibuya by describing the different attractions and places people can visit, and of course provide the google maps itinerary links.

The visitors of my website can access any necessary information about trains and such, in order to get around. Since as you may know traveling in japan is mostly by train

Need a hotel? Easy, here you'll find different hotels depending on prices with the possibility to book one either on my website with the form I coded or directly from Booking.com.

Everyone will be able to write comments, check the links I used to fetch my info and of course see my contact information at the bottom of the page.
